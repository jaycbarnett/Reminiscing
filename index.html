The Band

Bringing the Sunset Strip to Your Shore

Reminiscing is a six-piece tribute band devoted to the golden era of yacht rock — those impossibly silky grooves that drifted from Malibu to Marina del Rey between 1976 and 1984.

We faithfully recreate the lush arrangements, impeccable studio craft, and breezy charisma of legends like Christopher Cross, Hall & Oates, Little River Band, America, and more. Close your eyes, feel the salt air, and let the music take you out to sea.

From intimate supper clubs to sold-out festival stages, we bring authentic sound, period-correct wardrobe, and an infectious love for the music.

🎤 David Vece — Vocals / Sax / Guitar 🎸 Jay Barnett — Lead Guitar 🎼 Doug Smith — Bass 🥁 Tom Ward — Drums / Perc. 🎹 Steve Richards — Keys 🎤 Vicki Waves — Backing Vocals
